Zero

About Zero

Zero is an intoner whom is focused on killing her sisters in order to save the world and rid herself of the flower in her right eye. She is misunderstood in the beginning of the game but you slowly understand that she cares a lot more than she lets on. She is brave, confident. maybe even careless at times. This is mainly shown when she is so close to killing her sisters and another enemy appears to try and stop her. She hacks and slashes her way through them without a care.

Zero standing alone.

Zero's Characteristics

  1. She has a flower as an eye.
  2. She doesnt mind relying on others as long as they can fight and dont get in her way.
  3. She has a prosthetic arm.
